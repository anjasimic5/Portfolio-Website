About me

I am currently pursing a bachelor's degree in Computer Science at St. Lawrence University (SLU). Prior to coming to SLU, I studied at United World College of the Adriatic in Duino, Italy where I gained an IB Diplomma. I first started coding in high school in Serbia (Gimnazija "Svetozar Marković", Niš), where I learned object oriented programming in C++. My university experience has consisted of using Python, Java, JavaScript, and C while mastering classes in Data Structures, Web Programming, Algorithm Analysis, Computer Graphics, Artificial Neural Networks and Deep Learning, and more.
